## Summary of recommended parameter changes

- **`london_recency_penalty`** (currently 0.55): Cross-venue 1-5d overlap (all years) = **5.87%** (n=824); post-2024 = **3.64%** (n=293); post-2024 **5-day** = **9.64%** (n=61). Same-venue N1->N2 baseline = **0.74%**.
  At a 5-day cross-venue gap the actual repeat propensity is ~7-10%, **~10x** the same-venue baseline. A 0.55 multiplier excludes far too much - recommend **0.80-0.90** (gentle nudge) for the 5-day case. The model currently treats London just like a same-venue N1, which is wrong.

- **Anchor stickiness list** (rho=-0.5..-0.7). Since-2024 empirical anchors: strong (>=2 repeats) = **['Big Modern!']**; weak (1 repeat) = **['Echo of a Rose', 'Sirius', 'Green River', 'Slow Ready', 'Dramophone']**. Add to anchor list: **['Big Modern!']**. Drop (no evidence in any era): **['Hot Tea', 'Dripfield', 'The Empress Of Organos', "Don't Do It"]**. Important: Hot Tea, Dripfield, Madhuvan, Empress, Don't Do It have either never repeated or only 1x repeat across the entire data set - the *default* -0.95 stickiness already captures their non-repeating behavior. Special overrides may be overfitting to vibes.

- **`top_role_count`** (currently 8): set-2 opener top-8 share = 44.3% (top-12 = 58.0%); encore top-8 share = 44.0% (top-12 = 56.6%); opener top-8 share = 35.3% (top-12 = 46.7%). 
Top-8 is too narrow for some roles; consider top-10 or top-12.

- **`cover_appetite`** scaling target: last-50 cover rate = 88.0% (model assumes 88%). All-time = 92.5%. 
0.88 baseline is correct.

- **Marquee `cover_mult`** (currently 1.1): marquee cover rate = 88.9% vs last-50 baseline 88.0%. Empirical ratio = 1.01. 
Marquee shows are NOT cover-heavier than usual; consider 1.0.

- **`album_promotion`** (currently 1.5x): Post-release debuts/show = EMG 1.6, CYD 0.4; baseline last-50 = 0.36. 
Empirical lift ~2.8x is well above 1.5; raise to ~2.0-2.5.

- **Hot Tea slot bias**: empirical role mix = show_opener 6.7%, set1_closer 25.6%, set2_opener 4.3%, set2_closer 17.7%, encore 15.2%, other_mid_set 30.5%. 
The model should multiply Hot Tea's slot prior most heavily for **other_mid_set** (where it actually lives), not generic 'set-2 launch pad' alone.
